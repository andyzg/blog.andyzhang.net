Hello World,

This hour reminds of me of the time I was at a 21 hour hackathon.

Oh yes, EngHack. My team and I won 2nd place for that with our app called UWallet and it felt good to have accomplished something over the night. And day.

At the start, you feel driven, motivated, ambitious for a new product that is useful for the world. You start writing code like a monster, writing and writing. You are a machine. You get stuff done and you tackle part by part. Then morning hits. It's about 3:00am and you start losing focus. Caffeine wears out and you are stuck on the same error for 1 hour. True story, I spent 2 just to realize I forgot to add my event listener. What even..

But you reach a certain point where your body just wakes up again aka you were supposed to wake up. You start working again and you can see a good progress. You feel less tired because you're getting used to it.And then, time started passing so slowly and I was really unconscious of anything I was doing. Oh man, those were some unproductive hours. I still remember trying to fix a couple of bugs but overall, I took it easy. I managed to get a view nicer so that our app could display the transaction history properly. Efficiency was definitely not there though.

I'm tired now though. Good night world.
